---
title: "Rain Without Thunder"
date: "2025-9-23"

---


My ego whispered
that no one could love you as I did.
You buried that ego without a word,
simply by replacing me in ten days.

Why show me a world
that was never mine to keep?

Why plant a garden
in a soul
you never intended
to watch grow?

I tried to summon
a storm of hatred for you,
but my heart is not a storm cloud.
It can only rain.
So I cry for you,
and make you cry inside me.

Our love was a scene from a vintage movie.
The kind you replay in your mind,
knowing the ending will not change,
but hoping this time,
you will see the moment
it all began to fade.
